# Rejection Email Templates

Use these as starting points. Always personalize. Run through `rejection-writer` (mode 08) for role-specific versions.

---

## After Resume Screen (Brief)

**Subject:** Your application to [Role] at [Company]

Hi [First Name],

Thank you for your interest in the [Role] position at [Company] and for taking the time to apply.

After reviewing your background, we’ve decided to move forward with candidates whose experience more closely aligns with what we need for this role at this time.

We appreciate you considering [Company] as a potential employer and wish you the best in your search.

Warm regards,
[Recruiter Name]
[Company]

---

## After Phone Screen

**Subject:** Following up on your [Role] interview at [Company]

Hi [First Name],

Thank you for speaking with me about the [Role] position — it was great to learn more about your background.

After careful consideration, we’ve decided to continue with other candidates for this particular role. This was a difficult decision given the strength of the candidate pool.

[Optional: add one specific piece of genuine, constructive feedback here if appropriate.]

We’d love to stay in touch — your profile is a strong one and there may be a better-fit opportunity as we continue to grow.

Thank you again for your time and interest in [Company].

Best,
[Recruiter Name]
[Company]

---

## After Final Interview Round

**Subject:** [Role] at [Company] — Update

Hi [First Name],

Thank you so much for the time you invested in our interview process. Meeting with you and the team was genuinely valuable.

This was one of the most competitive searches we’ve run, and ultimately we’ve decided to extend an offer to another candidate whose background was a slightly closer match for [specific requirement or context — be honest but kind].

I want to be clear: this was not an easy decision, and the outcome reflects the strength of this particular candidate pool rather than a judgment on your capabilities.

I’d welcome staying in touch. May I connect with you on LinkedIn? I’d be happy to keep you in mind for future openings.

With appreciation,
[Recruiter Name]
[Company]
